ShrinkURL for iOS

The quickest and smartest way to shorten a URL on your iPhone,iPod Touch and iPad.

Copy a web link to the clipboard and let shrinkURL automatically convert to your chosen shortening service.

Save your favourite URLs to shrinkURLs history database for later retrieval.

Post them to Facebook, Twitter, Google Reader and more.


Roasting Pad for iOS

Roasting Pad gives you accurate cooking times for a selection of meats and cooking styles. Set the exact weight using imperial or metric and let Roasting Pad give you step by step instructions.

Swipe your fingers to turn pages while stepping backwards and forwards through the instructions and let Roasting Pad assist you with your cooking by setting alarms to remind you when to move on to the next step.


MotoCross Track, Sponsor & School Finder

RealMotocross, MX Track, School, Sponsor & Friend finder is a free way to find motocross related items.

Version 2.0 allows you to see status updates from other RealMotoCross members, view their Albums & Photos, see the latest Motocross events from around the world and view thousands of Categorised Moto Places in the palm of your hand.

It’s the only Motocross social network that shows you where to ride mx, where to train, how to get sponsored and helps you make friends so you never have to ride alone.


Posterous Safari Extension

Being a fan of the Posterous platform and a Mac user, I thought I would take time out to develop a Safari Extension of my own.

I’ve wrapped up the official Posterous bookmarklet in to a Safari toolbar item, all the functionality of the brilliant bookmarklet remains but having a neat little toolbar icon seems just that little bit more elegant. If you would like to use it for yourself then you can download it from here.
